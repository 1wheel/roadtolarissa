Left circle spins, playing a WebAudio cord corresponding to the highlighted blocks (first version).

Every 7 beats, the right circle advances 1/9th of a rotation. Every blue highlight block on the right circle swaps the on/off status of the corresponding block on the left circle. Remove blocks flash red, added block green.

Click a block to toggle its state

Every 8*7*9 beats left untouched, the state of the left circle will flip. It will return to its original state after 1004 beats
