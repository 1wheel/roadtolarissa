The M.T.A. announced November TKTK that the 7 train's new, computerized signal system was activated.

To fix the subway, the M.T.A. plans to spend $20 billion in over ten years to modernize the signals across the system. Each line will have years of weekend and overnight closures.

This could be difficult. The 7's signal upgrades ran years behind schedule and over budget.
